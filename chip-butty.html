Chip Butty Bosh!


Butty

Chips. Bread. Red or Brown. No other way.

Lunch is ready

Ingredients

  • Bap or Bread (Cheap)
  • Salted Butter
  • Finest Grade thick cut chips
  • Gravy Granules
  • Sea Salt
  • Red or Brown sauce

Let's Get Cooking

  1. Get that oven on and blast those chips. We want them nice and crispy for this recipe.
  2. (Optional step)
    Scrap cooking chips yourself and support a local business and buy some chips at your local chippy.
  3. Slap those chips onto a slice of heavily buttered bread, as many as you can stack.
  4. Squeeze a load of your preferred sauce on, and chuck that last slice of bread on.

    5. Presentation

    • Place on chopping board
    • Half with sharp knife to see it's heavenly glory
    • Eat straight at kitchen side no need to sit down
